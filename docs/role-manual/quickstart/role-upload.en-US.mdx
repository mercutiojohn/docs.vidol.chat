## Upload Steps

### Step 1: Open the Character Interface

1. Click on the `Character` tab.
2. Select the character you want to upload from the list on the left.

<Frame>
  ![Open Character Interface](https://oss.vidol.chat/assets/b8f35ef6504c9724ae7c402f9da24c11.webp)
</Frame>

### Step 2: Share to Assistant Market

1. Click the `Share to Assistant Market` button.
2. In the pop-up modal, add a globally unique character identifier.
   - You can specify it manually.
   - Alternatively, you can use the random generation button to create one automatically.
3. Click the `Submit Assistant` button, and the system will upload the model and avatar-related files.

<Frame>
  ![Share to Assistant Market](https://oss.vidol.chat/assets/e82d86015fdc8cec9f3da066119e11b4.webp)
</Frame>

<Note>
  Please ensure that your character identifier is globally unique to avoid conflicts with other characters.
</Note>
